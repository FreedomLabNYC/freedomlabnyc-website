What is Freedom Lab?

Freedom Lab is a NYC group for freedom tech enthusiasts to come together to learn, build, and explore technologies that preserve our digital sovereignty. Whether you're a beginner trying their first piece of software or experienced developer, into Bitcoin, privacy, or open-source solutions, this is where we can all work to become freer together.

Volunteer

Paid membership helps our freedom tech community grow, but isn't the only way to get involved. We also welcome volunteering such as:

  • Finding more spaces to host with us
  • Teaching classes and hosting events
  • Video editing and social media
  • Software engineering and hardware
  • Any other skills you'd like to bring!
